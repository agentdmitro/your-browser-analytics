# Privacy Policy for Your Browsing Analytics

Last updated: 2026-01-11

## Overview

Your Browsing Analytics is a Chrome extension that analyzes your browsing history
locally and provides visual insights into your web usage patterns. This policy
explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Access

The extension accesses:

- Browsing history (URLs, page titles, visit timestamps) via the History API

## Data Processing and Storage

- All processing happens locally in your browser.
- Data is not sent to any external servers.
- The extension does not store data outside of your device.
- Exported data is created only when you explicitly use the export feature.

## Data Sharing

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Permissions

The extension requires permissions to read your browsing history in order to
generate analytics and visualizations.

## Your Choices

You can remove the extension at any time to stop all data access and processing.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on the project
repository.
